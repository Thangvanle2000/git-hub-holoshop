Return Information

30 Day No Sweat Returns

Holoshop wants you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Items can be returned at any point, within 30 days from the original invoice date.

  • To receive an exchange or refund for the full value of your purchase, products must be returned in new. Please note that while we want you to be happy with your purchases
  • Products returned in used or worn condition within 30 days from the original invoice date can be exchanged for another item.
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable.

Free Return Shipping

Free return shipping is only available for orders shipped within the 48 hours in India.

A complimentary Holoshop return label is included in every package delivered within the 48 hours in India. Please allow 7-10 business days for delivery back to Holoshop.
